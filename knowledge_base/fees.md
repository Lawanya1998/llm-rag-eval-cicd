# NimbusPay — Fees

NimbusPay uses a simple fee structure.

- Sending money to another NimbusPay user is **free**.
- Sending money to an external bank account costs **1.5%** of the amount, capped at **$10**.
- Instant transfers carry an additional **0.5%** fee on top of the standard fee.
- Currency conversion adds a **2%** margin on the mid-market exchange rate.
- There is **no monthly account fee** on personal accounts.
- Business accounts pay a flat **$15 per month** plus standard transfer fees.

ATM withdrawals are free for the first **$400** per month, after which a **2%** fee applies.
