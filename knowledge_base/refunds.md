# NimbusPay — Refunds Policy

NimbusPay customers can request a refund within **30 days** of a transaction.

- Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.
- Standard refunds are processed within **5 to 7 business days**.
- Transactions over **$2,000** require identity re-verification before a refund is approved.
- Subscription payments are refundable only if cancelled within **14 days** of renewal.
- Refunds cannot be issued for cryptocurrency purchases, which are final.

To request a refund, open the NimbusPay app, go to **Activity → select the transaction → Request Refund**.
