# NimbusPay — Accounts & Limits

NimbusPay offers two personal tiers and one business tier.

- **Basic** accounts require only an email and phone number, with a sending limit of **$1,000 per week**.
- **Verified** accounts require government ID and raise the limit to **$25,000 per week**.
- **Business** accounts require company registration documents and have **no fixed weekly limit**, subject to review.

Account holders must be at least **18 years old**. NimbusPay is currently available in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom.

A single user may hold **one personal account and one business account**. Opening multiple personal accounts is not permitted and may result in suspension.
