# NimbusPay — Security & Account Safety

NimbusPay protects accounts with several security features.

- Two-factor authentication (2FA) is **required** on all accounts.
- Biometric login (fingerprint or face) is supported on mobile devices.
- If suspicious activity is detected, the account is **temporarily frozen** and the user is notified by email and push notification.
- Users can freeze their card instantly from the app under **Card → Freeze**.
- NimbusPay staff will **never** ask for your password or one-time code by phone or email.
- Card details are protected using tokenization, so merchants never see your real card number.

If you believe your account is compromised, contact support immediately at **support@nimbuspay.example** or through in-app chat, available **24/7**.
